# BMR Roadmap

Future improvements and upcoming features in the **BETTERMEETINGROOMS** platform.

This roadmap provides a high-level overview of what is coming next across the four phases of the BMR Room Lifecycle Solution:  
**Discovery, Design, Deployment, and Maintenance.**

Features are grouped into:
- **Short-term:** November 2025 – January 2026  
- **Medium-term:** February – April 2026  
- **Long-term:** 3–12 months

## 📅 February – April 2026

**Measurement location detection in the acoustic flow**  
  Measurement and speaker positions will be stored and visualized inside the Digital Twin.

**Acoustic material needed to improve room acoustics**  
  Calculate required acoustic treatment based on room size, geometry, and selected catalog supplier.

**Web-based 3D Designer**  
  Edit AR and 3D designs directly in the web editor using Discovery data.

**Digital Twin Creator**  
  Create time-stamped Digital Twins representing historical snapshots of a room.

**Catalog builder for Organisation Admins**  
  Build and manage custom equipment catalogs using 3D models.

**Room versioning system**  
  Full versioning of Designs and Digital Twins with historical comparison and rollback.

**Auralizations of rooms**  
  Experience how a room sounds before and after acoustic treatment.
