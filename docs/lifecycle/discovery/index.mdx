# <Icon name="search" size="1em" /> Discovery Phase

Using just an iPhone, you can perform a guided acoustic measurement grounded in peer-reviewed research — a repeatable, comparable result every time, no specialist equipment needed. The same workflow captures a precise 3D scan and documents key room elements.

The **Discovery Phase** is the first step in the BETTERMEETINGROOMS lifecycle. The data you capture becomes the foundation for every phase that follows, and is stored in the room's **Room Data**.

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> Room Data

All data collected during Discovery is stored in the **Room Data** section of the room in the app:

| Data | Source |
|---|---|
| 3D scan geometry | Room Scan |
| Markers & photos | Marker placement |
| Acoustic profile | Acoustic Measurement |

Room Data becomes the foundation used throughout Design, Deployment, and Maintenance.

---

## <Icon name="alert-triangle" size="1em" /> Prerequisites & Tips

- ✅ iPhone or iPad with **LiDAR** (iPhone 12 Pro or newer, iPad Pro 2020 or newer)
- ✅ Good lighting helps the scan — avoid scanning in very dark rooms
- ✅ Run the acoustic measurement **before** requesting a design — placement decisions improve significantly

> The more complete the Discovery data, the faster and more accurate the Design phase will be.
