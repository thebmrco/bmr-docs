# <Icon name="3d-ar-design" size="1em" /> AR Design Guide

Place equipment directly into the physical room using augmented reality on your iPhone or iPad. The result is saved as a design and combined with the room scan — ready to continue refining in BMR Studio.

---

## <Icon name="alert-triangle" size="1em" /> Before You Start

A **room scan is required** before starting an AR Design. The scan provides the coordinate space that links AR-placed items to the room model. If no scan exists, the app will prompt you to scan the room first.

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> After Saving

Once uploaded, the AR Design appears in the room's **Designs** section alongside any designs created in BMR Studio. You can:

- Open it in **BMR Studio** to add more equipment, adjust placement, or refine the layout
- Compare it with other design versions for the same room
- Move it through approval and mark it as the **Active Design** when installed

---

## <Icon name="star" size="1em" /> Tips

- **Scan first, design second** — the scan is essential. Without it, AR-placed items cannot be combined with the room model and the design cannot be saved.
- **Good lighting** helps the app find the scan coordinates faster.
- **Walk around the room** slowly during the coordinate-finding step — the app needs to recognise surfaces from the original scan.
- **Place items one at a time** — position each piece before adding the next for the most accurate result.
- AR Design works best for **initial placement and visualisation**. For detailed configuration, cable routing, and coverage analysis, continue in BMR Studio.
