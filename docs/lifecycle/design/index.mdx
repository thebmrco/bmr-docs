# <Icon name="design" size="1em" /> Design Phase

The **Design Phase** turns your Room Data into a complete, validated room setup. BMR offers two design tools — **BMR Studio** for detailed 3D design work on any device, and **AR Design** for placing equipment directly in the physical room using augmented reality.

---

## <Icon name="palette" size="1em" /> BMR Studio

The unified 3D workspace for all room design work. Available on **Web**, **iPhone**, and **iPad**.

<img
  src={useBaseUrl('/img/design/bmr-studio-ui.png')}
  alt="BMR Studio — 3D room design environment"
  style={{width: '100%', borderRadius: '10px', border: '1px solid #E5E5D5', margin: '1rem 0', boxShadow: '0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)'}}
/>

BMR Studio uses dedicated **modes** — View, Structurals, Equipment, and Cables — so you can focus on one thing at a time. Place equipment from the catalog, adjust room structure, plan cable routing, create multiple design versions, and mark the final design as **Active**.

**[Read the full BMR Studio guide &rarr;](bmr-studio)**

---

## <Icon name="3d-ar-design" size="1em" /> AR Design

Place equipment directly into the physical room using augmented reality on your iPhone or iPad. Items are positioned at real scale in the actual space, and the result is saved as a design that you can continue refining in BMR Studio.

AR Design requires a **room scan first** — the scan provides the coordinate space that links AR-placed items to the room model. Once saved, the AR Design appears alongside Studio designs and can be opened in BMR Studio for further work.

**[Read the full AR Design guide &rarr;](ar-design-guide)**

## <Icon name="help-circle" size="1em" /> Request Design Assistance

If you prefer to have BMR handle the design, you can request assistance through our website. A BMR designer will use your Room Data to produce a complete design package, which is then uploaded to the room for your review.

**What you receive:**
- Full design with placed equipment
- Equipment list with key components
- Acoustic treatment recommendation
- Coverage visuals (camera, audio, screen) where applicable

To request assistance, visit **[bettermeetingrooms.com](https://www.bettermeetingrooms.com)** and use the **Request Assistance** option.

---

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> Outputs of the Design Phase

- An **approved design** with a complete equipment list
- **Coverage visuals** — camera view, screen visibility, audio coverage
- **Acoustic recommendation** based on measurements and use case
- A **full version history** tied to the room

---

## <Icon name="alert-triangle" size="1em" /> Prerequisites

- ✅ Completed **scan** and **markers** — Room Data ready
- ✅ **Acoustic measurement** uploaded (strongly recommended)

> The better the Room Data, the faster the Design — and the fewer revisions needed.
