# <Icon name="design" size="1em" /> Design Phase

The **Design Phase** turns your room's data into a complete, actionable setup plan — a vision of **what the room could be**. Designs are built on top of the **Room Data** collected during Discovery: the scan, markers, and acoustic measurements.

---

## <Icon name="palette" size="1em" /> BMR Studio Designer

**BMR Studio Designer** is the primary way to create designs. It is a web-based 3D environment where you plan, place, and configure everything that should go into a meeting room — working directly on top of the Digital Twin.

BMR Studio Designer is focused on **what the room could be**: proposing layouts, selecting equipment, and producing a complete design ready for deployment.

:::info
BMR Studio Designer lives in the **Design** section of a room. Its counterpart — [BMR Studio Editor](../digital-twin/index.mdx) — lives in the Digital Twin and is used to document the room as it currently exists.
:::

### What you can do in BMR Studio Designer

- **Place equipment** from the catalog — cameras, screens, speakers, codecs, furniture, and more
- **Position and orient** items precisely within the 3D room model
- **Manage cables** between devices
- **Switch between versions** to compare design options
- **Review coverage** — camera view, screen visibility, and audio coverage overlays
- **Generate an equipment list** automatically from placed items

### How to open BMR Studio Designer

1. Open the room on the **web platform**
2. Navigate to **Design**
3. Click **Open Studio Designer** — the 3D editor loads with your Digital Twin

:::tip
The richer your Discovery data — scan quality, marker coverage, acoustic measurements — the more accurately Studio Designer reflects the real room.
:::

## <Icon name="help-circle" size="1em" /> Request Assistance

If you prefer to have BMR handle the design, you can request assistance through our website. A BMR designer will use your Room Data to produce a complete design package, which is then uploaded to the room for your review.

**What you receive:**
- Full design with placed equipment
- Equipment list with key components
- Acoustic treatment recommendation
- Coverage visuals (camera, audio, screen) where applicable

To request assistance, visit **[bettermeetingrooms.com](https://www.bettermeetingrooms.com)** and use the **Request Assistance** option.

---

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> Reviewing & Approving a Design

Whether created in BMR Studio Designer or delivered by BMR, the review process is the same:

1. Open the room's **Design** section to view available designs
2. Inspect the 3D layout, coverage visuals, and equipment list
3. Add comments or request adjustments
4. **Approve** the design when ready to move into Deployment

---

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> Outputs of the Design Phase

- An **approved design** with a complete equipment list
- **Coverage visuals** — camera view, screen visibility, audio coverage
- **Acoustic recommendation** based on measurements and use case
- A **review trail** tied to the room

---

## <Icon name="alert-triangle" size="1em" /> Prerequisites

- ✅ Completed **scan** and **markers** — Digital Twin ready in Room Data
- ✅ **Acoustic measurement** uploaded (strongly recommended)

> The better the Room Data, the faster the Design — and the fewer revisions needed.
