## üì± App (iPhone / iPad)

<details class="bmr-details">
<summary class="bmr-summary">New Acoustic Flow</summary>

A completely new **Acoustic Flow** has been introduced to guide users step by step through acoustic assessment of a room.

The flow is designed to be **guided, repeatable, and easy to understand**, helping users perform reliable acoustic measurements directly in the app.

### Step-by-step Acoustic Flow

#### 1. Room Setup & Measurement Guidance
Users are guided on how to position the phone and speaker correctly before starting the measurement.

![Room setup guidance](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-01.jpg)

---

#### 2. Device Selection
The app clearly shows the selected **input device (microphone)** and **output device (speaker)** to ensure correct setup.

![Device selection](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-02.jpg)

---

#### 3. Volume Adjustment & Validation
Users are guided to adjust speaker volume to the recommended level.  
The app validates the difference between ambient noise and speaker level before allowing the measurement to continue.

![Adjust volume](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-03.jpg)

---

#### 4. Measurement Preparation
Clear instructions ensure users remain still and quiet during the measurement to guarantee accurate results.

![Measurement preparation](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-04.jpg)

---

#### 5. Measurement in Progress
Visual feedback confirms that the measurement is ongoing and prevents accidental interruptions.

![Measuring in progress](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-04-2.jpg)

---

#### 6. Results & MOS Score
After completion, users receive:
- A **MOS (Mean Opinion Score)**  
- An acoustic summary (e.g. RT60)  
- Frequency-based measurement graphs  

This makes it easy to understand room acoustic quality at a glance.

![Measurement results](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-05.jpg)

---

### Key Benefits
- Structured, guided acoustic measurements  
- Reduced risk of incorrect setup  
- Clear, understandable results  
- MOS score based on research with our university partner  
- Integrated seamlessly into the Discovery phase
