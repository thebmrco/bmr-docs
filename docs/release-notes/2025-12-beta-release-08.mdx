# Release 8 (BETA) 2026 January

This release introduces a major new **Acoustic Flow** alongside improvements to room progress visibility, scanning experience, and collaboration workflows — across both the **iOS App** and the **Web Platform**.

---

## <Icon name="smartphone" size="1em" /> App (iPhone / iPad)

<details className="bmr-details">
  <summary className="bmr-summary">
    <Mic size={18} color="#206B31" style={{marginRight: "8px"}} />
    New Acoustic Flow
  </summary>

A completely new **Acoustic Flow** guides users step by step through acoustic assessment of a room.

The flow is designed to be **guided, repeatable, and easy to understand**, helping users perform reliable acoustic measurements directly in the app.

### Step-by-step Acoustic Flow

#### 1. Room Setup & Measurement Guidance

Users are guided on correct phone and speaker positioning before starting the measurement.

![Room setup guidance](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-01.jpg)

---

#### 2. Device Selection

The app clearly displays the selected **input device (microphone)** and **output device (speaker)**.

![Device selection](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-02.jpg)

---

#### 3. Volume Adjustment & Validation

Users adjust speaker volume to the recommended level.  
The app validates ambient noise vs speaker level before proceeding.

![Adjust volume](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-03.jpg)

---

#### 4. Measurement Preparation

Clear instructions ensure stillness and silence during the measurement.

![Measurement preparation](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-04.jpg)

---

#### 5. Measurement in Progress

Visual feedback confirms the measurement is active and prevents accidental interruption.

![Measuring in progress](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-04-2.jpg)

---

#### 6. Results & MOS Score

After completion, users receive:

- **MOS (Mean Opinion Score)**
- Acoustic summary (e.g., RT60)
- Frequency-based measurement graphs

This makes acoustic quality understandable at a glance.

![Measurement results](/img/release/2026-01-Acoustic-flow-05.jpg)

### Key Benefits

- Structured, guided acoustic measurements  
- Reduced risk of incorrect setup  
- Clear and understandable results  
- MOS score based on research with our university partner  
- Seamlessly integrated into the Discovery phase
