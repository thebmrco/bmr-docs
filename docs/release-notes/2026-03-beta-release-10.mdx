# March 2026 – Release 10

This is a major release. The BMR App is now available on the **App Store**, the room home screen has been fully redesigned, and the acoustic measurement flow has been rebuilt from the ground up with multi-position guidance and speaker photo capture.

---

## <Icon name="star" size="1em" /> App Store Availability

The BMR App is now distributed through the **Apple App Store** — no more TestFlight.

Users still require an **invitation link** from the BMR team to access the app after installation. Once installed, the app works the same way — the invitation link unlocks the account and organisation access.

This is a major milestone for the platform, making distribution significantly simpler and more reliable for all users.

The room home screen has been completely redesigned. Three top-level tabs now structure all room content:

- **Overview** — key room info and a summary of all captured data at a glance
- **Room Data** — all data gathered from the room (scans, markers, acoustic measurements)
- **Designs** — all design versions associated with the room

**What you see on Overview:**

- Floor area and AR world map status
- Who added the room and when
- Acoustic MOS score (latest measurement)
- Count of Scans, Markers, and Acoustic measurements — with the date of the last entry and a shortcut to add more

The acoustic measurement flow has been rebuilt as a fully guided, 5-step process. The biggest change is that measurements are now taken at **three positions** in the room, giving a significantly more accurate and representative acoustic picture.

**Step 1 — Device selection**

Select the input (microphone) and output (speaker) devices. The app confirms the device position has been found.

**Step 2 — Room setup & speaker photo**

The app shows a 3D illustration of the room with placement guidance: keep the speaker at least **1 m / 3 ft away from the wall**. You are then prompted to **capture a photo of the speaker** — align the crosshair with the speaker and tap to capture.

**Steps 3 & 4 — Measurements at three positions**

The app guides you to measure at positions **a**, **b**, and **c** — each shown on a 3D room illustration. Stand at the indicated position and tap **Start Measurement**. Progress is tracked as you complete each position. You can choose to finish early with fewer measurements if needed.

<div style={{display:'flex', gap:'1rem', margin:'1rem 0'}}>
  <img src="/bmr-docs/img/release/2026-03-Acoustic-measurement-a.jpg" alt="Measurement at position a" style={{width:'50%', borderRadius:'12px'}} />
  <img src="/bmr-docs/img/release/2026-03-Acoustic-measurement-b.jpg" alt="Measurement at position b" style={{width:'50%', borderRadius:'12px'}} />
</div>

**Step 5 — Results**

After all measurements are complete, the app shows the Mean Opinion Score, average RT60 based on all positions, and a full RT60 graph across frequency bands. Toggle between the combined average and individual measurement positions. Tap **Save Results** to store in Room Data.
