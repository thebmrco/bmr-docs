# Welcome to BETTERMEETINGROOMS

**BETTERMEETINGROOMS** is a platform that helps organizations plan, design, and manage high-performing meeting rooms. Our app supports the full lifecycle of a meeting space — from the first scan to long-term maintenance.

---

## <Icon name="lifecycle" size="1em" /> What We Offer

We guide you through the **three key phases** of meeting room projects:

<LifecycleFlow />

### <Icon name="search" size="1em" /> Discovery
Capture and understand the current state of the room using LiDAR scans, markers, and acoustic measurements. All data is stored as **Room Data** — the foundation for every phase that follows.

### <Icon name="design" size="1em" /> Design
Build and refine the room in **BMR Studio** — a unified 3D workspace. Place equipment from the catalog, review coverage overlays, compare design versions, and mark the final design as **Deployed** — the authoritative record of the room as installed.

### <Icon name="wrench" size="1em" /> Maintain
Keep rooms performing long after installation. The Deployed Design provides the context for troubleshooting, upgrades, and ongoing lifecycle management — all connected to the room's complete history.
