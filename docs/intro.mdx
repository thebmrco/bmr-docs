# Welcome to BETTERMEETINGROOMS

**Meeting room expertise, at scale.**

Getting meeting rooms right has traditionally required specialist expertise — acoustic consultants, AV designers, dedicated resources. That works for flagship rooms, but most organizations manage tens or hundreds of spaces without the tools to replicate that standard consistently.

**BETTERMEETINGROOMS** puts expert-level room measurement and design within reach of every project — using nothing more than a mobile phone. No specialist equipment, no consultant required on-site. Any team member can capture a precise 3D scan, run a scientifically validated acoustic measurement, and document key room elements in a single guided workflow.

---

## <Icon name="lifecycle" size="1em" /> What We Offer

We guide you through the **three key phases** of meeting room projects:

<LifecycleFlow />

### <Icon name="search" size="1em" /> Discovery
Capture and understand the current state of the room using LiDAR scans, markers, and acoustic measurements. All data is stored as **Room Data** — the foundation for every phase that follows.

### <Icon name="design" size="1em" /> Design
Build and refine the room in **BMR Studio** — a unified 3D workspace. Place equipment from the catalog, review coverage overlays, compare design versions, and mark the final design as **Active** — the authoritative record of the room as installed.

### <Icon name="wrench" size="1em" /> Maintain
Keep rooms performing long after installation. The Active Design provides the context for troubleshooting, upgrades, and ongoing lifecycle management — all connected to the room's complete history.

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> What You Achieve

**Acoustic quality you can prove.**
MOS scores and RT60 values give every room a measurable benchmark — before and after, comparable across your entire portfolio. Visual graphics show the **Green Zone** target area.

**Fewer surprises, lower cost.**
Structured data and verified designs replace guesswork. Get deployments right first time and reduce the cost of rework and revisions.

**One record for every room.**
IT, facilities, and support teams all work from the same live source of truth — what is installed, where, and how it connects.

**Expert standards, at any scale.**
Any team can assess and document any room. Green Zone benchmarks then ensure every deployment meets the same defined quality bar — across 10 rooms or 10,000.
