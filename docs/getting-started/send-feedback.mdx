# <Icon name="comments" size="1em" /> Send Feedback

Anywhere in BMR, you can send the team a **Bug report**, **Feature request**, **Question**, or **Other** message — directly from the product. It is the fastest way to get something in front of the BMR team without leaving the app.

Called **Send Feedback** in the BMR App (under **Settings**) and **Report an Issue** on the Web (in the **header**). Both open the same form with the same four categories.

---

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> Where to find it

The same form is available on both surfaces, but lives in a slightly different place:

| Platform | Label | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **Web** (`app.bettermeetingrooms.com`) | **Report an Issue** | In the **header bar**, next to the room search and notification bell. Available from any page. |
| **BMR App** (iPhone / iPad) | **Send Feedback** | Open **Settings** and tap **Send Feedback**. It is not pinned in the header — you reach it from Settings. |

Either entry point opens the same form, and reports land in the same queue with the BMR team.

---

## <Icon name="comments" size="1em" /> What you can send

When you open the form you pick one of four categories:

| Category | When to use it |
|---|---|
| **Bug** | Something is not working the way you expect — a button does nothing, data looks wrong, a flow breaks. |
| **Feature Request** | Something is missing or could be better. Use this for "It would be great if BMR also did X." |
| **Question** | A how-to or clarification — "Can BMR do X?" or "How should I handle Y?" |
| **Other** | Anything that does not fit the categories above. |

Each report needs a short **summary** (one-line subject) and a longer **description**. The description is free text — write whatever helps the team understand what happened or what you want.

---

## <Icon name="map-pin" size="1em" /> Scoping the report

Below the description you can optionally tell us **what the report relates to**:

- **Organization** — applies to a whole organisation
- **Location** — applies to a specific site or building
- **Room** — applies to a single room

Scoping is optional, but it is very useful for bug reports — it lets the BMR team open the same room or scan that you were looking at when you noticed the issue.

---

## <Icon name="star" size="1em" /> Tips for a useful report

- **Be specific.** "Acoustic measurement keeps cancelling at step 3 with JBL Xtreme 4" is far easier to act on than "acoustic broken".
- **Say what you did and what you expected.** A short before/after is usually all the team needs.
- **Mention device and version when reporting a Bug.** "iPad Pro 12.9" (2022), release 1.2" gives the team something to reproduce against.
- **For Feature Requests, describe the underlying need.** Knowing *why* you want a feature helps the team design the right thing, not just the literal request.
- **Scope it to the room when relevant** — especially for Bug reports tied to specific data.
