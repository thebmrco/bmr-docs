# Account Setup

Follow the steps below to get access to BETTERMEETINGROOMS and start using the platform.

---

## <Icon name="key" size="1em" /> Step 1: Request Access

Contact us at **support@bettermeetingrooms.com** to request access. Once approved, you will receive an **invitation email** containing:

- A **link to download the app** from the Apple App Store
- A **link to sign up** and set your password

---

## <Icon name="mail" size="1em" /> Step 2: Set Your Password

Open the sign-up link from the invitation email and create your password. Use your **company email address** — this ensures you are placed in the correct organisation and can see the same rooms and data as your colleagues.

:::caution Important
If you use a private email address (e.g., Gmail, Outlook, iCloud) you will not be able to access the same resources as your colleagues. Always use your company email.
:::

---

## <Icon name="smartphone" size="1em" /> Step 3: Logging In

Once your password is set, you can log in via the **App** or the **Web**. Both use the same Welcome screen for authentication.

### Via the App

Download the app from the **App Store** using the link in your invitation email and tap **Login** to be taken to the Welcome screen.

  { label: "Tap Login to begin", image: "/img/account-setup/Account-setup-app-1.png", alt: "App Login Screen" },

  { label: "Done — you are now logged in and ready to go" },

### Via the Web

Go to **[app.bettermeetingrooms.com](https://app.bettermeetingrooms.com)** — you are taken directly to the Welcome screen. Enter your email and password to log in. The web platform gives you access to room management, acoustic comparisons, and **BMR Studio**.

---

## <Icon name="circle-check" size="1em" /> After Logging In

You can now access the app or the web platform and start setting up rooms.

> Make sure your company has already been set up in BMR. If not, contact your project lead or email **support@bettermeetingrooms.com**.
