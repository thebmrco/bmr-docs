# <Icon name="help-circle" size="1em" /> Frequently Asked Questions

---

### <Icon name="key" size="1em" /> Account & Access

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> How do I sign up?

**BMR Mobile** is available on the **Apple App Store**. You still need an **invitation link** from the BMR team to unlock account and organisation access — contact us at **support@bettermeetingrooms.com**. Once invited, you can sign in with your company email and password, or via Google or Cisco Webex SSO.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Can I use a personal email (like Gmail or Outlook)?

You can, but if you are part of a company you will not see the same rooms and organisation data as your colleagues. Use your company email to be placed in the correct organisation.

---

### <Icon name="smartphone" size="1em" /> Devices & Requirements

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Which devices support room scanning?

iPhones and iPads with **LiDAR** — iPhone 12 Pro or newer (Pro / Pro Max), iPad Pro 11" (2nd gen, 2020) or newer, iPad Pro 12.9" (4th gen, 2020) or newer. See [System Requirements](system-requirements) for the full list.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Can I access BMR on the web?

Yes. Go to **[app.bettermeetingrooms.com](https://app.bettermeetingrooms.com)** — the web platform is where you manage rooms, compare acoustic measurements, run organisation-wide search, and work in **BMR Studio**, the 3D environment for design and the Active Design record.

---

### <Icon name="lifecycle" size="1em" /> Lifecycle Phases

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> What is the Discovery Phase?

Discovery is where you capture the room with an iPhone — a scan, markers and photos, and a guided acoustic measurement. All of this becomes the room's **Room Data**, which every later phase builds on.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> What is the Design Phase?

Design happens in **BMR Studio** on the web, iPhone, and iPad. You can design the room yourself — placing equipment from the catalog, reviewing coverage overlays, and managing design versions — or use [Send Feedback](send-feedback) to ask the BMR team a question or request specific help with a design.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Is BMR Studio available to me?

Yes — BMR Studio is in **Beta** but is enabled for everyone. It works on the **web**, **iPhone**, and **iPad**. Open any room, go to **Design**, and click **Open Studio** to get started.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Can I add notes to rooms and other elements?

Yes. You can add notes to organisations, locations, rooms, scans, markers, acoustic measurements, and designs — on both the web platform and the app. Notes are visible to all team members with access.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> What is a Default Room Configuration?

A recommended equipment setup defined by your organisation admin, based on room size and usage type. When you design a room in BMR Studio that matches a configuration's size range, the recommended equipment appears in the sidebar — ready to place. See [Default Room Configuration](../lifecycle/design/default-room-configuration) for details.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> What is Maintenance?

Maintenance uses the **Active Design** — the live record of the room as installed — as a single source of truth that **IT, facilities, and support teams** can all work from, without manual upkeep. Live monitoring, troubleshooting workflows, and upgrade requests are on the roadmap.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> How do I move a room to a different organisation or location?

Open the room and go to the **Overview** page. Tap **Edit** to enter edit mode — you can then select a new organisation and location for the room. Save your changes to apply the move.

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> How do I delete a location?

Open the **Organisation** page and go to the **Locations** tab. Each location entry has a **garbage bin** icon — tap it to delete that location.

### <Icon name="alert-triangle" size="1em" /> Platform Limitations

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Do you support Android devices?

No. The app runs only on iOS devices with LiDAR. The BMR web platform works in any modern browser — see [System Requirements](system-requirements).

---

### <Icon name="mail" size="1em" /> Support

#### <Icon name="circle-question-mark" size="1em" /> Who do I contact if I get stuck?

Use **[Send Feedback](send-feedback)** to send a Bug report, Feature request, Question, or Other message directly to the BMR team. It is in the **header** on the Web (labelled *Report an Issue*) and under **Settings** in BMR Mobile (labelled *Send Feedback*). The team monitors this channel actively, so it is the fastest way to get a reply.
