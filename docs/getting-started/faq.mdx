## <Icon name="key" size="1em" /> Account & Access

**How do I sign up?**

The BMR App is available on the **Apple App Store**. You still need an **invitation link** from the BMR team to unlock account and organisation access — contact us at **support@bettermeetingrooms.com**. Once invited, you can sign in with your company email and password, or via Google or Cisco Webex SSO.

**Can I use a personal email (like Gmail or Outlook)?**

You can, but if you are part of a company you will not see the same rooms and organisation data as your colleagues. Use your company email to be placed in the correct organisation.

## <Icon name="smartphone" size="1em" /> Devices & Requirements

**Which devices support room scanning?**

iPhones and iPads with **LiDAR** — iPhone 12 Pro or newer (Pro / Pro Max), iPad Pro 11" (2nd gen, 2020) or newer, iPad Pro 12.9" (4th gen, 2020) or newer. See [System Requirements](system-requirements) for the full list.

**Can I access BMR on the web?**

Yes. Go to **[app.bettermeetingrooms.com](https://app.bettermeetingrooms.com)** — the web platform is where you manage rooms, compare acoustic measurements, run organisation-wide search, and work in **BMR Studio**, the 3D environment for design and the Active Design record.

---

## <Icon name="lifecycle" size="1em" /> Lifecycle Phases

**What is the Discovery Phase?**

Discovery is where you capture the room with an iPhone — a scan, markers and photos, and a guided acoustic measurement. All of this becomes the room's **Room Data**, which every later phase builds on.

**What is the Design Phase?**

Design happens in **BMR Studio** on the web. You can design the room yourself — placing equipment from the catalog, reviewing coverage overlays, managing design versions — or [request assistance](../lifecycle/design/) and have a BMR designer prepare the design for you.

**How do I request design assistance?**

Visit **[bettermeetingrooms.com](https://www.bettermeetingrooms.com)** and use the **Request Assistance** option. A BMR designer will use your Room Data to produce a complete design package, which is uploaded back to the room for review.

**Is BMR Studio available to me?**

Yes — BMR Studio is in **Beta** but is enabled for everyone. Open any room on the web, go to **Design**, and click **Open Studio** to get started.

**What is Maintenance?**

Maintenance uses the **Active Design** — the live record of the room as installed — as a single source of truth that **IT, facilities, and support teams** can all work from, without manual upkeep. Live monitoring, troubleshooting workflows, and upgrade requests are on the roadmap.

## <Icon name="alert-triangle" size="1em" /> Platform Limitations

**Do you support Android devices?**

No. The app runs only on iOS devices with LiDAR. The BMR web platform works in any modern browser — see [System Requirements](system-requirements).
